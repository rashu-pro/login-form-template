Help & Support

If you haven't received the password reset email or your email isn't working properly, you may need to take additional actions. Please see the following tips to successfully reset your password.

I haven't received any password reset email

The password reset link can take up to 15 minutes to arrive. If you happen to not get any password reset links, please wait for some time and also check your email’s "Trash" or "Spam" folder.

I am facing trouble receiving password reset link

If you have any trouble receiving your password reset link, please contact our support team. Call us at +13178540207 or send an email to support@masjidsolutions.net.

I forgot my email address

If you are not able to log in with your email, that means your account is not in the system or you forgot the registered email address.

Please contact us at +13178540207 or send an email to support@masjidsolutions.net regarding this issue. We will fix the problem or help you create another account with a different email.
